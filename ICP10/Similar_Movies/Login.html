Our team name is the HolyHandGenades and we are working on the AR: Well-being Tracker for the Propel Progam. This application will be a mobile app for ios and android phones. It is partially a chatting application where students can message their mentor directly and other students. Using AR technology students will interact with an avatar and answer daily survey questions. There will be a form option for students who prefer not to use the AR interface. Points will be given to students for every survey completed. This point total will be displayed along with student achievements on their user profile. Lastly a news section will be available, displaying news and events related to UMKC.
